About Cocky Mocky

Who We Are.

CockyMocky is an innovative beverage catering company based in Nairobi, Kenya and the surrounding areas. We cater for all corporate and private functions within Nairobi and outside Nairobi. The unique feature of CockyMocky is that it is able to cater for all your bar needs regardless of the location; from rooftops, beach resorts, indoor arenas or just the simple birthday party at your home. CockyMocky Drinks was established in 2016 by young individuals with over 3 years experience in beverage services from recognized 5 star hotels and bars in the country hence confidence in our service delivery from our highly trained Barmen. Our main aim is to excite and engage with our clients in a reputable manner as well as offering services that get attention and become a talking point. We aspire to go further in the world of cocktails and the events industry hence constantly coming up with fresh ideas to ensure we meet the clients' expectations.With our cocktail event services, we not only prepare extravagant and classic cocktails but we also deliver them in a professional and most reliable way. We are keen towards the latest cocktail trends and usually empasise on fresh seasonal ingredients; we can mix up anything from old classics to wild new creations that will keep your guests chatting, dancing, and also coming back for more. We offer services from as low as 10 pax to 400 pax based on clients' needs.

Our Services

We offer exceptional services ranging from:

Cocktails

We strongly believe that your party deserves style & polish. We think your event, regardless of the reason you are celebrating, should come as close as possible to flawless, seamless, smooth, relaxed and fun. It’s not just about mixing drinks and pouring the perfect glass of beer, it’s about becoming an extension of the atmosphere you are creating. We believe that the bar is the hub of the party: information central, the core, the heart, and the pivotal location that almost every single guest will visit at least once during their time at your party. We know that the experience they have at the bar, will be a reflection on you.

Corporate Events

CockyMocky bar service can be customized to provide the perfect bar for your party. We offer a full range of beverage and bartending services designed to make your event a success. We can set up a complete, well-stocked bar for your event and staff it with our professional bartenders. We know that professional event bar service adds polish and the highest level of customer service to your event. Knowing that your bar is in good hands lets you relax and enjoy your event. Our event bar service professionals can assist you in putting together a full bar for any size event, make wine selections to fit any budget.


Wedding Ceremonies

Your wedding is one of the most important days of your life. You will invest a great amount of time and money into planning the perfect party for your friends and family, so why take chances on your bar service- an important piece of the magic? We know just how important your special day is to you. CockyMocky offers an array of full-service packages that are well-suited to making your day stress free and ensure that your guests will have a great time. Not only do we offer competitive pricing, but we don't charge you any extra because it's a wedding.

Private & Birthday Parties

Our mobile bar hire package is the perfect solution to your birthday & Private party dilemma. Whether you are having a small celebration in your back garden, or maybe your looking for the ultimate birthday celebration, our mobile bar services will make your birthday a night to remember. A good bar caterer like CockyMocky can help you determine how much alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages you need for your party. Having someone sort out your bar needs, makes you available to your guests and allows you to enjoy the party.
