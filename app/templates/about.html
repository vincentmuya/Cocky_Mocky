Our History

CockyMocky is an innovative beverage catering company based in Nairobi, Kenya and the surrounding areas. We cater for all corporate and private functions within Nairobi and outside Nairobi. The unique feature of CockyMocky is that it is able to cater for all your bar needs regardless of the location; from rooftops, beach resorts, indoor arenas or just the simple birthday party at your home. CockyMocky Drinks was established in 2016 by young individuals with over 3 years experience in beverage services from recognized 5 star hotels and bars in the country hence confidence in our service delivery from our highly trained Barmen. Our main aim is to excite and engage with our clients in a reputable manner as well as offering services that get attention and become a talking point. We aspire to go further in the world of cocktails and the events industry hence constantly coming up with fresh ideas to ensure we meet the clients' expectations.With our cocktail event services, we not only prepare extravagant and classic cocktails but we also deliver them in a professional and most reliable way. We are keen towards the latest cocktail trends and usually empasise on fresh seasonal ingredients; we can mix up anything from old classics to wild new creations that will keep your guests chatting, dancing, and also coming back for more. We offer services from as low as 10 pax to 400 pax based on clients' needs.
